Mock practical exam without database:

Build a course registration page.

Requirements:

1. Use `session_start`.
2. Store courses in a PHP array.
3. Show a POST form with:
   - student name
   - level radio buttons: regular or employee
   - course checkboxes
4. Validate:
   - student name is required
   - at least one course must be selected
5. Calculate:
   - total credits
   - total fees
   - 20% discount if level is employee
6. Save each successful registration in `$_SESSION["history"]`.
7. Display the history in a table.
8. Add a clear history link.
